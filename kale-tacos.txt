Ingredients:

1 onion
about 25 small soft taco shells
3 avocados
1 bunch kale
1 can black beans
1 can refried beans
coconut oil
olive oil
salt
pepper
cayenne pepper
1-2 Tablespoons cumin
1 Tablespoon Jamaican allspice
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
paprika


Directions:

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.

2. Saute the onion in coconut oil for five minutes, add two cans of 
beans, salt, pepper, a few dashes of cayenne pepper, cumin, garlic 
powder, and the magic ingredient: Jamaican allspice. Cook about 15 
minutes.

3. Cut one bunch of kale into bite-sized pieces and place in a large 
mixing bowl. Mix in olive oil or avocado oil, salt, pepper, a dash of 
cayenne pepper, a dash of paprika. Mix thoroughly.

4. Arrange the taco shells in a U shape in a baking pan.

5. In each taco, add a large spoonful of beans.

6. Then add a layer of kale.

7. Bake at 350 for about 20 minutes until the taco shells start to get a 
little crispy.

8. Cut up your avocados and put them inside the tacos.
